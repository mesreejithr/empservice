Frequently Asked Questions

Most asked questions about Hireclap Recruitment Solutions

How can Hireclap make my hiring process easier and more effective?

Hireclap transforms your hiring process into a seamless experience. We connect you with 3M+ jobseekers and 12,000+ colleges across India through our comprehensive platform. From expert-assisted hiring to AI-powered interviews, we handle every aspect of recruitment. Our smart tools help you source, assess, and hire the right candidates faster than traditional methods.

Who should use Hireclap's recruitment services?

Hireclap is ideal for companies hiring fresh graduates, entry-level professionals, or running large-scale campus or off-campus drives. Whether you're a startup or a large enterprise, we provide solutions tailored for bulk hiring, role-based hiring, and skill-based evaluations.

What is included in Hireclap's Assisted Hiring service?

Our Assisted Hiring includes expert-led job postings, application sourcing, candidate shortlisting, and interview coordination. You pay only for shortlisted candidates you're interested in—making the process faster, efficient, and performance-driven.

Can I manage hiring myself using Hireclap?

Yes. With our Recruitment Packages, employers can post unlimited jobs, access a fresher candidate database, and manage the hiring process independently—perfect for organizations that prefer hands-on control.

How do Hireclap's Online Assessments improve candidate selection?

Our platform, AssessEngine, allows you to conduct skill-based and aptitude assessments with anti-cheating measures like webcam monitoring. It helps filter top candidates early, saving time and improving hiring accuracy.

What makes Hireclap's video interview platform different?

Our AI-powered video interviews offer smart scheduling, real-time AI question prompts, whiteboard collaboration, and post-interview insights. It ensures structured, unbiased, and efficient interviews—anytime, anywhere.

How quickly can I start receiving applications?

Once your job is live, our targeted campaigns can start bringing in relevant applications within 24–48 hours. With assisted hiring, we ensure faster application turnaround and quality candidate engagement.
